Who We Are

We provide free education to underprivileged children and help build a better future through learning and support. Over the years, we have reached hundreds of students, helping them take meaningful steps toward their dreams. Each child we support represents a story of hope, resilience, and potential. We work closely with families and local communities to create a supportive environment where children can thrive both inside and outside the We also believe in transparency, accountability, and impact. Every contribution we receive is carefully utilized to maximize its benefit for the students we serve. Our supporters and donors are an essential part of our journey, helping us expand our reach and deepen our As we continue to grow, our vision remains clear: to build a world where no child is left behind due to lack of resources or opportunity. Together, we can make a lasting difference—one student, one story, and one future at a time.

Christian Public Model School is dedicated to providing quality education rooted in strong moral values and discipline. We aim to develop confident, responsible, and capable individuals ready to face real-world challenges.

Our approach combines academic excellence with character building, ensuring students grow not only intellectually but also morally and socially.
