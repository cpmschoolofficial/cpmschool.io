About Our School

Who We Are

Christian Public Model School is committed to providing free education to underprivileged children. Since 1997, we have been helping families by offering quality education, discipline, and strong moral values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide free education, basic needs, and a safe environment where every child can learn, grow, and succeed in life.

Our Vision

We aim to build a society where every child has access to education, opportunity, and a better future regardless of financial background.
