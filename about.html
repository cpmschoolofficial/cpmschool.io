About Our School

About Admissions

Christian Public Model School provides quality education for all children. Our admission process is simple, transparent, and designed to support underprivileged families. We welcome students from all backgrounds and ensure equal learning opportunities.

Who We Are

📘 About School

Christian Public Model School is committed to providing high-quality education, strong moral values, and a bright future for every student.

🎯 Our Commitment

We believe in excellence, discipline, innovation, and character-building. Our experienced teachers help every child succeed.

🤝 Community Support

We are helping poor children who cannot afford education and currently using church facilities to provide free learning.

🎓 Mission

To provide free education, basic needs, and a safe environment for underprivileged children.

🌍 Vision

To create a society where every child has access to education and opportunity.

📜 History

We have been running this school since 1997 to help poor people grow through education.
