About Our School

Who We Are

Christian Public Model School is committed to providing high-quality education, strong moral values, and a bright future for every student. Our mission is to help students grow academically, socially, and personally.


We believe in excellence, discipline, innovation, and character-building. Our experienced teachers and modern facilities support every child’s success.

Christian Public Model School is dedicated to providing free education to children who cannot afford schooling. We are currently operating in a church environment and helping poor families build a better future.

Our aim is not only education but also character building, discipline, and moral values. We believe every child deserves a chance to learn and succeed in life.

Mission

To provide free education, basic needs, and a safe environment for underprivileged children.

Vision

To create a society where every child has access to education and opportunity.

History

We have been running this school since 1997 with a vision to help poor people grow through education.
